### **TYPography SUMMARY**

#### Font Hierarchy:
1. **Hero Heading** (Serif, 3em+, 700-800 weight) - Most prominent
2. **Section Headings** (Serif, 2em, 700-800 weight) - Secondary prominence
3. **Article Titles** (Sans-serif, 1.2em-1.3em, 600-700 weight) - Tertiary
4. **Body Text** (Sans-serif, 1em, 400 weight) - Standard reading
5. **Small Text** (Sans-serif, 0.8em-0.9em, 400 weight) - Supporting info

#### Color Palette:
- **Primary Text**: Dark blue/black (#1a1a1a or similar)
- **Secondary Text**: Dark grey (#666666 or similar)
- **Background**: Light beige/off-white (#f5f5f0 or similar)
- **Accents**: Orange, yellow, blue (used in images and buttons)

#### Design Principles:
- **Contrast**: Strong contrast between serif headings and sans-serif body text
- **Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy through size and weight variations
- **Readability**: Comfortable font sizes following web accessibility standards
- **Consistency**: Consistent font families within text categories

---

### **RECOMMENDATIONS FOR REPLICATION**

To replicate this design style:

1. **Navbar Logo**: Use a two-weight approach - lighter for "International", bolder for "Civil Society Centre"
2. **Headings**: Use a serif font (Playfair Display, Lora, or similar) at 700-800 weight
3. **Body Text**: Use a clean sans-serif (Open Sans, Lato, or Roboto) at 400 weight
4. **Font Sizes**: Maintain the hierarchy: 3em+ for hero, 2em for sections, 1.2em for cards, 1em for body
5. **Images**: Use abstract, collage-style illustrations with warm color palettes (oranges, yellows, blues)
